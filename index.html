INTRODUCING

TVL Chit Fund (P) Ltd.

TVL Chit Fund (P) Ltd., was started in 1991 and got registered in 1999 in a humble way, with the sole aim of serving the unbanked people in and around Guntur City. Today, its activities are spread throughout Andhra Pradesh and Telangana, serving clients of diverse back grounds. It takes care of the needy - the poor, lower and the upper middle classes; of course the rich as well, with the spontaneous response from the public.

The Company’s day to day administration is handled by Sri. Tavva Venkateswarlu, Managing Director & Foreman, Smt. Tavva Sujatha, the Director.

Background Of

Chit Fund Business

It is unique to South India, an indigenous financial institution till 1970’s; Commercial banks were not accessible to ordinary people in those days. To meet the emergencies, unforeseen expenditures, house purchase and marriage of children etc., one has to approach the traditional money lenders. The interest rates are exhorbitant and many a time, the borrower is subjected to humiliation by the lender for failing to repay the interest and principal amount in time. Nationalization of Banks didn’t improve the situation. Chit Funds proved to be an alternative source of lending and savings. The activities of the chit fund companies are monitored by Government through the Chit Fund Act of 1982.
